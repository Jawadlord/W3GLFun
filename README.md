## ğŸ® Game Rules

### Roulette
- Players place bets on specific numbers, colors, or ranges.
- The wheel spins and the ball lands on a number.
- Payouts depend on the bet type (e.g., straight-up, split, color).
- House edge applies; always gamble responsibly.

### Slot Machine
- Players insert credits or tokens and spin the reels.
- Wins occur when matching symbols align on paylines.
- Payouts vary based on symbol combinations.
- Features like bonus rounds may be included.

### Blackjack
- Players aim to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding.
- Cards are dealt; players can hit, stand, double down, or split.
- The dealer plays after players; higher hand wins.
- Fairness is guaranteed through blockchain verification.

### General Rules
- All bets are final once placed.
- Misuse or cheating will result in disqualification.
- Responsible gaming is encouraged.
